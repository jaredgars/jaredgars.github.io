Jared Gars

I am a researcher at the University of Chicago and UCEMA as part of JILAEE – the Joint Initiative for Latin American Experimental Economics and LEAP, Bocconi.

I will join the Food and Resource Economics Department at the University of Florida as Assistant Professor in January, 2022.

Previously, I was a Junior Economist in the Trade and Agriculture Directorate at the OECD.
